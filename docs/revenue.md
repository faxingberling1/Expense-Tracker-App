# Revenue Model

## Pricing Strategy

Use freemium with a generous free tier. The free product must be useful enough to build trust before asking users to pay.

## Free Tier

Included:

- Manual expense tracking.
- Limited SMS/notification extraction.
- Voice expense logging.
- Basic timeline.
- Basic categories.
- Monthly totals.
- Limited receipt scans.

## Premium Tier

Included:

- Unlimited SMS/notification extraction.
- Unlimited receipt scans.
- AI assistant.
- Monthly Rewind.
- Financial Health Score details.
- Spending predictions.
- Subscription leak detector.
- Smart budget suggestions.
- Cloud backup.
- CSV/PDF exports.
- Advanced goals.

## Family Tier

Post-MVP:

- Shared household dashboard.
- Spouse/roommate budgets.
- Shared grocery and bill tracking.
- Split expenses.
- Shared goals.

## Suggested Pricing

Pakistan pricing should be localized.

Possible starting point:

- Monthly: PKR 499-799
- Annual: PKR 4,999-7,999
- Family monthly: PKR 999-1,499

Pricing should be tested against willingness to pay. Students and young professionals may need lower annual pricing or launch discounts.

## Paywall Timing

Do not paywall basic tracking too early.

Good paywall moments:

- User asks AI assistant a deeper question.
- User wants full monthly Rewind.
- User hits receipt scan limit.
- User wants prediction or subscription detector.
- User wants cloud backup or export.

## Strongest Premium Hook

The strongest premium bundle is:

> AI insights + predictions + subscription leak detector + rewind.

This package sells clarity, not bookkeeping.
